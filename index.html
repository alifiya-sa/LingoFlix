The problem

Language apps are broken.

  • ✕

  • ✕
    Most learners plateau at intermediate because they never practice in real-world contexts
  • ✕
    Generic lessons feel boring and repetitive — one-size-fits-all doesn't work for language
  • ✕
    Apps are siloed — media, partners, and structured study all live in different places

Process

From install to fluent

01

Install the extension

Add LingoFlix to Chrome in seconds. It overlays seamlessly on top of your favorite streaming platforms.

02

Watch anything

Choose your target language and press play. LingoFlix activates subtitles, highlights key vocab, and tracks what you're learning.

03

Practice with AI

Pause at any moment to have a real conversation with our AI about what just happened in the show — in your target language.

04

Connect with partners

Match with native speakers watching the same content. Co-watch, chat, and reinforce learning through real human connection.

Browser extension

Passive watching becomes active learning

No need to leave Netflix or YouTube. LingoFlix sits as an invisible layer and activates the moment you press play.

  • ✓Interactive subtitles — tap any word for instant definition, pronunciation, and example sentences
  • ✓AI conversation mode — discuss scenes, ask questions, and get corrections in real time
  • ✓Vocabulary bank — every word you learn is tracked and built into spaced-repetition flashcards
  • ✓Adaptive difficulty — the extension detects your proficiency and adjusts the challenge automatically
